About me.

I am a beginner UX designer who wants to create products that solve user problems. The most interesting part of design for me is the analysis of user and business needs and building UX strategy. I believe that in order to find good solutions it is necessary to ask the right questions, listen to users, observe their behavior and finally understand their needs. And above all of that I’m optimistic design thinker.
