About the project

The SGH student chooses the subjects, instructors and arranges the schedule of classes (based on the study guide and the information contained therein). This is called semester declaration to be completed within strictly defined deadlines. It is very important to complete the compulsory subjects for a given field of study.

The problem to work through is the difficulty that students find in choosing the right lecturer and interesting subjects.

The assumption of the project being implemented is to create a tool to facilitate finding a subject and teacher appropriate for the student.

UX strategy

Purpose.

To make it easier for students
to choose the right lecturers and subjects

Target group.

Students of all faculties
and types of studies at SGH.

Student surveys.

Surveys conducted using a Google form. survey

Most students look for information about the subject and teacher before enrolling in it.

Over 40% of people have problems finding interesting information.

Students are mainly influenced by the opinion of others. They look for information on Facebook and in the study guide.

The most important for students is: way of conducting classes, rules for completing the course, an approach leading to students, practical use of the subject

Building the UX strategy, I conducted a SWOT analysis, defined the goal using the S.M.A.R.T model and tried to propose solutions through Value Proposition Canvas.

Then I moved to Business Model Canvas, which I think is a very useful tool in creating UX strategies. It combines the conclusions of all the methods used.
